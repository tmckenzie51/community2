Step Two: Create Your Community

You can create your own Community to find others who share your Community. At Community we know that you are a dynamic, multi-dimensional person. We recognize that even within groups that we identify ourselves with, that there are many other nuances of experiences, that make us all unique individuals with unique experiences. In keeping this in mind, we allow you to create as many communities as you'd like, within your broader communities.

Step Three: Connect with Others In Your Community

After creating your Community you will receive a feed of matches, as well as be added to a storyboard where Community members share their own personal stories for provding inspiration, motivation and for putting a smile on your face. Feel free to add your story to the storyboard and be inspirared by the stories of others.
